About Me

My journey into technology began in high school, where I completed foundational computer
science courses at Saraswati Higher Secondary School. I'm currently in my second year at the
Japan International Institute of Cybernetics (日本国際工科専門学校), specializing in IT Management,
and I'm expected to graduate in March 2026.

In 2018, I started working as a sales representative, where I developed strong communication
and problem-solving skills. During that time, I became increasingly interested in design and
development, which sparked a deeper interest in building websites and digital experiences.
I started taking on small freelance projects to practice and improve my skills.

I'm actively expanding my skills as a developer. I'm currently focused on HTML, CSS, and
Java, while also learning JavaScript and Python.

I genuinely enjoy learning and love building clean, high-performance web experiences.
I'm passionate about continuous growth and excited about where this journey in tech will
lead me.
